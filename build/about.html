Who we are

We are an organization that consists of people who love children and wants to stand next to children suffering from cancer but also to their families. Our work started in 2012 and our base is in Athens.

What we do

  1. Financial support for families
  2. Medical care
  3. Visit of nurses at home
  4. Psychological support
  5. Provision of shelter near to the hospital for the families of the child's
